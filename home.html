Welcome to SOS Children's Village in South Africa

Our goal is to help orphaned and abandoned children find a place to call home. Many people don’t realize that a child’s sense of culture and belonging comes from a place where their basic needs are met, including food, healthcare, and shelter. SOS Children’s Village in South Africa provides these children with a safe environment, allowing them to escape loneliness and experience the joys of childhood—playing and engaging in activities that children with parents often take for granted.
